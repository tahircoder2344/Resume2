Educational History

I successfully did my Matriculation from The Causeway School, Lahore and went for further studies to Cadet College Sohawa. There, I completed my Intermediate in Medical Studies with fine grades. I also embarked the rewarding post of Hafiz-e-Quran from Jamia Madina-tul-Ilm and became a Hafiz.

From Medical to Coding

As stated earlier, I did my intermediate in Medical Studies but after completing it, I went for MDCAT Exam and scored good marks but until then, I was very well aware about the merit formulating system and exam checking and taking system. I knew that I am not being made for further Medical Study. Then with some counselling and encouraging statements, I choosed the field of Computing and Technology. And started learning coding and programming skills. And I am still in Progress

Why Coding?

The ability to shape user experiences through intuitive interfaces, seamless functionalities, and robust architectures is what drives my fascination. The creative freedom within coding allows me to sculpt ideas into tangible entities, breathing life into concepts and transforming them into functional, user-centric solutions. Embracing the ever-evolving landscape of web technologies, I find a profound joy in unraveling the intricacies of programming languages, mastering frameworks, and leveraging these tools to craft innovative digital experiences.
